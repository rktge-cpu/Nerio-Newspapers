FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQ) for customers about our premium WordPress themes and plugins.

?
What is RSTheme and what makes it unique?

RSTheme is a cutting-edge premium WordPress themes and plugins Development framework designed to empower users with a seamless and customizable website-building experience. Its uniqueness lies in its robust features, user-friendly interface, and commitment to staying ahead of the latest industry trends.

?
How frequently are updates released for RSTheme?

We are dedicated to providing regular updates to ensure compatibility with the latest WordPress versions, security patches, and to introduce new features. Updates are typically released quarterly, and users will receive notifications within the WordPress dashboard.

?
How do I obtain the purchase code from Themeforest?

To get your purchase code from Themeforest, log in to your Themeforest account, navigate to the “Downloads” section, locate the RSTheme purchase, and click on it. On the product page, you’ll find the “License certificate & purchase code” option. Download the text file, and your purchase code will be inside.

?
How to activate by purchase code?

After obtaining the purchase code, go to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to RSTheme settings, and find the “License” tab. Enter your purchase code in the designated field and click “Activate.” This process ensures that you receive timely updates and access to premium support.

?
Do I need coding skills to use RSTheme's Plugin?

No coding skills are required to use RSTheme’s Plugin. With its intuitive drag-and-drop interface and user-friendly design options, you can effortlessly customize your website without any coding knowledge. RSTheme’s Plugin is designed to cater to both beginners and experienced developers.

?
Is RSTheme suitable for beginners ?

RSTheme caters to both beginners and experienced developers. With its intuitive drag-and-drop interface, beginners can easily create stunning websites without coding. Advanced developers can leverage the powerful customization options and extend functionality through the built-in developer-friendly tools.

?
Can I customize the design and layout of my website with your themes?

Absolutely! RStheme offers extensive customization options, allowing you to control the layout, colors, fonts, and other design elements. The drag-and-drop builder makes it easy for users to create a website that reflects their brand or personal style without the need for coding.

?
How to setup demo homepage and blogpage ?

Setting up demo pages is easy with RSTheme. Navigate to the theme settings in your WordPress dashboard, find the “Demo Import” section, and select the desired demo content. With just a few clicks, you can import the demo homepage and blog page, allowing you to start with a professionally designed foundation.

?
WordPress backend coming without any style?

If the WordPress backend lacks styling, it may be due to a caching issue. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the problem persists, check for conflicts with other plugins or themes. Updating your browser and WordPress to the latest versions can also resolve styling discrepancies.

?
Are you failing to import demo data?

If you encounter issues importing demo data, ensure that your server meets the minimum requirements for RSTheme. Check your internet connection, and if you’re using a firewall or security plugin, temporarily disable it during the import process. For more detailed troubleshooting, refer to the documentation or reach out to our support team for assistance.
